Ruby Business UK

Ruby Business UK is a regular daytime meetup for Rubyists. It happens on the second Wednesday of every month at the Lantana Shoreditch, near Old Street Tube Station.

Please register to the next meetup at the Ruby Business (UK) Meet Up Group.

Let’s get together and chat about best practice Ruby development, and do some good old fashioned business networking. There’s no set agenda: just turn up, grab a coffee and discuss Ruby in the business community. Mostly for Ruby consultants and owners of software development shops, although any people interested in the subject are welcome. See you at 11am at the Benugo Bar!

Find us on Meet Up, Twitter and Google Groups.
